## Consulting Services

I take on a focused number of consulting engagements per quarter. Typical engagements range from **$150–$250/hr** depending on scope and urgency. Retainer arrangements available for ongoing regulatory or AI governance support.

### 📄 Technical Writing for Life Sciences
Expert scientific and regulatory documentation for companies under FDA oversight — from 510(k) premarket notifications and IND/NDA/BLA submissions to REST API reference documentation, clinical study protocols, investigator brochures, and standard operating procedures. Every deliverable is authored with firsthand knowledge of what FDA reviewers, IRBs, and technical auditors actually scrutinize.

### 🤖 AI Integration Consulting
End-to-end AI strategy, implementation planning, and governance framework development for pharmaceutical and biotech organizations deploying LLMs (including Claude and GPT-4) in drug discovery, clinical operations, and regulatory affairs. Work product is designed to be defensible to FDA inspection and compliant with 21 CFR Part 11, GxP validation requirements, HIPAA, and the EU AI Act.

### ✅ Regulatory Compliance Consulting
Gap assessments, compliance audits, and remediation roadmaps for FDA-regulated systems and processes — including 21 CFR Part 11 electronic records and electronic signatures, HIPAA Privacy and Security Rules, GxP computerized system validation (GAMP 5), and ICH E6(R3) GCP compliance for clinical trials.
